About Taylor

I always struggled with knowing what I would do for my career. Switching from many subjects, I was constantly looking for what would be my "spark." In high school, I had a few coding classes, remembered how much I enjoyed them and downloaded a coding app. I had finished at least three more coding games and three free courses pretty soon. I loved it! I then joined a slack coding group where I began talking to others about their journey/work, and it made me even more excited to meet future developers with who I can work together and learn.

As a student at Thinkful, I've begun my journey to becoming a software developer and couldn't be happier!

If you're looking to hire a new developer please reach out! I am so excited to see what web development has in store. I have always wanted to change the world through my creative and genuine hard work and passion, and now I have the opportunity to do so.
